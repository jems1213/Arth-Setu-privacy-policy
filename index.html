2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide and information generated when you use the App.

2.1 Account and profile information

  • Email address — for sign-in (email one-time password) and account identification.
  • Name — for display on your profile and in shared groups.
  • Profile photo (optional) — if you choose to upload an avatar.
  • Phone number (optional) — only if you choose to add it to your profile; not required for basic sign-in.
  • Authentication identifiers — internal user IDs from our authentication provider when you sign in.

2.2 Financial and app usage data

  • Expenses and income — amounts, categories, dates, notes, tags, and related metadata you enter or import.
  • Budgets and goals — limits, targets, and progress you configure.
  • Shared groups (Splitwise-style) — group names, members, shared expenses, settlements, and balances.
  • Wallets / accounts — labels and balances you manage in the App.
  • Recurring expenses — schedules and amounts you set.
  • Receipt or attachment images (optional) — photos you attach to expenses.
  • Categories — default and custom categories you create.
  • Reports and analytics — derived summaries computed from your data inside the App.

2.3 Device and technical data

  • Push notification token (FCM) — so we can deliver budget alerts, group activity, and other notifications you enable.
  • Device type and OS version — as part of standard app and notification services.
  • App version — for support and compatibility.

2.4 SMS Hub (optional, on your device)

If you grant SMS permission and use SMS Hub, the App reads messages from your device’s SMS inbox to detect bank and UPI-style debit/credit transaction messages.

  • Parsing and matching happen on your device.
  • Raw SMS message content is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Only parsed fields you choose to import (such as amount, date, and description) become expense records stored in your account.
  • Pending SMS suggestions may be stored locally on your device until you import or dismiss them.
  • You can disable SMS access at any time in your device settings or turn off SMS Hub in the App.

The App does not use SMS to send messages, act as your default SMS app, or intercept incoming SMS in the background beyond the optional inbox scan you enable.

2.5 Voice entry (optional)

If you use voice features, the App may use your device’s microphone to capture speech for expense entry or commands. Audio is processed for transcription on the device or via speech services as needed to fulfill the feature. We do not use the microphone when you are not actively using voice features.

2.6 AI assistant (optional)

If you use the in-app assistant, your questions and relevant financial context from your account may be processed to generate responses. Do not share sensitive information you do not want processed for this feature.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • Store, sync, and display your expenses, budgets, goals, and group data.
  • Send push notifications you opt into (budget warnings, group updates, reminders).
  • Provide SMS Hub import suggestions (on-device only).
  • Generate reports, charts, and insights inside the App.
  • Improve reliability, security, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with applicable law.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Third-party services

The App relies on trusted third-party providers. They process data only as needed to provide their services to us.

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and cloud data storage (including profile, expenses, budgets, groups, and file attachments). Account data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on Supabase infrastructure.

4.2 Google Sign-In

If you choose Continue with Google, Google authenticates you and provides an ID token to our backend. Google’s use of data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

4.3 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications to your device. Firebase may receive device tokens and message delivery metadata. See Firebase privacy information.

4.4 Other processors

Email delivery for sign-in codes and account-related messages may be handled through our authentication and email infrastructure. We do not share your data with advertisers.

5. Data storage, security, and retention

  • Encryption in transit: Data sent between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Local storage: Session tokens and sensitive local data may be stored using encrypted storage on your device.
  • Retention: We retain account data while your account is active. After you delete your account, we remove or anonymize your profile and associated data from our servers within a reasonable period, except where we must retain limited information for legal or security purposes.
  • Security: We use industry-standard measures, but no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong sign-in method and protect your device.

We may share information only in these cases:

  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, or security.
  • Business transfer — if the App or its assets are transferred, subject to this policy or notice to you.
